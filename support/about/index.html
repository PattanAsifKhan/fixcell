About Us

At FIXCELL we strive to help people by providing extraordinary service, complete transparency in the work we do and customize the user phone as one desires too.

WHO ARE WE?

We are a group of young group enthusiastic individuals who have a keen sight of mobiles and technology. We are the tech-maniac's or gadget gurus you hear about. Give us a chance to serve you and you'll understand why we are the best.

WHAT DO WE DO?

We undertake phone repairs. We come to your place at your convenient timing, fix your broken phone in your presence.Whatever situation your phone might be into we are ready to fix it. At Fixcell, customer satisfaction plays a major role.

WHY WE DO IT?

We do it becuase we are in love with phones. We love what we do and we are doing our part in keeping the world connected. In Abstract,We are the tech lovers. Join the community, help us to help you.

Core Values

Everybody has standards ours are just higher.

Care for People
People > Tech

Not only do we respect technology, but most importantly we care about the people using it. We put our customers’ satisfaction above anything else.

Work with Passion
We love fixing things

Anyone can say that they love their job, but do they have the attitude to back it up? At Fixcell we are passionate about our work and that passion can be felt in every repair that we do.

Focus on Quality
We take pride in our work

We believe that a repair is only as good as the quality of the parts used. Some repair companies will cut corners by using inferior glass or they buy parts from an unknown origin. We stand by every repair that we do and the parts that we use.

Make it Better
Constantly Innovating

We don’t need to tell you that technology is constantly changing. That’s why we are always on the cutting edge. We can’t predict that every house will have robot butlers, but if it happens you can bet that we’ll be ready to fix your phone if something goes wrong!

Stay Humble
Never Stop Learning

We’re always striving to be better and we see every opportunity as a learning experience for us. We know that we may not have all the answers, but that doesn’t stop us from trying.
